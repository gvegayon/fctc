Support the implementation and enforcement of smoke-free policy in Vietnam using the network of Vietnam Public Health Association

Vietnam Public Health Association

The aim of this project is to strengthen the implementation and enforcement of Vietnam's smoke free policy in government offices, health, education and public transportation facilities in two provinces. It builds upon accomplishments in 6 other provinces and will build enforcement capacity of local leaders and designated enforcement offices in Thua Thien - Hue and Khanh Hoa provinces through provision of toolkits, guidelines, training and technical support. The project will also increase awareness and support for enforcement through networking, engaging the media, and educating and informing the public and policy-makers. The project also seeks to build capacity for stronger enforcement of national smoke free laws.
